Awarded 2026–27 Cockrell Academic Development Funds with Lance Manuel for **UT STEER** (Structural Thinking through Exploration, Experimentation, and Reasoning).

The project develops `ce329ai`, an open-source library combining a structural-reasoning prompt layer, a rule-based validator, an LLM, and a 2D/3D analysis engine.
