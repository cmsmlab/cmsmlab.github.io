Gave a talk at the [20th U.S. National Congress on Theoretical and Applied Mechanics (USNCTAM 2026)](https://www.usnctam26.org/home) in Pasadena, CA:

- **B. Dortdivanlioglu, A. Umurhan, J. Wang** — *Large Strain-Gradient Effects on Bound Charge Evolution and Electric Potential in Dielectric Elastomers*  

The talk presented a multiscale framework linking the molecular design of polymers—through tailored dipolar characteristics and copolymer architectures—to macroscopic polarization that emerges under large strain gradients in deformation-driven instabilities.
