We presented our research at the 17th U.S. National Congress on Computational
Mechanics (USNCCM) held in Albuquerque, New Mexico.

Ashkan gave a presentation titled "Stabilized Material Point Method for
Self-Contacting Porous Hydrogels," and Berkin talked about modeling
viscoelasto-capillary deformations in soft materials.
