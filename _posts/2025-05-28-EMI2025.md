Gave a talk at the **Engineering Mechanics Institute Conference (EMI) 2025** in Anaheim, CA:

- **B. Dortdivanlioglu, J. Wang** — *Size-Dependent Instabilities in Highly Deformable Strain-Gradient Materials*

The work behind this talk is led by PhD student Jun Wang and develops a strain-gradient framework for predicting how length-scale effects shift the onset of instabilities in soft polymeric solids.
